Spacing System

  • Primary lockup symbol height is slightly larger than wordmark cap height: the approved premium ratio.
  • Horizontal gap is based on creator-node geometry: approximately 0.42x the node diameter.
  • Clearspace is at least 0.5x the creator-node diameter on all sides.
  • Use simplified favicon below 32px; do not use the full app icon at 16px.
  • Keep neon green as symbol/accent color; keep wordmark white/bone on dark or near-black on light.
